<h3>🏠 About Me</h3>

<p>📢 Hi! I am <strong>Fu Hu (伏虎)</strong>, a Ph.D. candidate at <strong>Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST)</strong>, advised by <strong>Prof. Yihua Tan</strong>.</p>

<p>🔱 My research focuses on <strong>Multi-Agent Reinforcement Learning</strong>, including cooperative policy learning, knowledge routing, parameter sharing, representation disentanglement, and quality-diversity learning. I am particularly interested in building efficient cooperation mechanisms for complex multi-agent systems.</p>

<p>🛠️ I also enjoy building simulation and embodied intelligence systems with <strong>Unity</strong>, <strong>ROS</strong>, <strong>NVIDIA Isaac Sim</strong>, <strong>OpenCV</strong>, and UAV platforms. Before my doctoral research, I worked on wearable-sensor-based monitoring for Parkinson's symptom fluctuations.</p>

<p>😊 Feel free to contact me by email if you are interested in discussing or collaborating with me.</p>
